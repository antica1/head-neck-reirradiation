# Quad-Shot + Pembrolizumab — JAMA Otolaryngol 2026 Evidence Summary

> PMID: 42313403 | JAMA Otolaryngol Head Neck Surg. 2026

## Trial Design

- Nonrandomized clinical trial
- Pembrolizumab 200 mg Q3w + Quad-Shot RT (3.7 Gy × 4 fractions, BID over 2 days, repeated every 4 weeks, up to 3 cycles)
- Population: Recurrent, unresectable, or metastatic HNSCC

## Key Findings

- Quad-Shot + IO shows improved outcomes vs IO monotherapy in advanced HNSCC
- Low-dose, hypofractionated RT enhances ICI efficacy through immunogenic cell death
- Q4w spacing allows for T-cell expansion between radiation bursts

## Quad-Shot Prescription

| Parameter | Value |
|-----------|-------|
| Dose per fraction | 3.7 Gy |
| Fractions per cycle | 4 (BID, ≥6h interval) |
| Cycle length | 4 weeks |
| Maximum cycles | 3 |
| Total physical dose | 44.4 Gy (if 3 cycles) |
| Combined with | Pembrolizumab 200 mg Q3w |

## Immunological Rationale

- 4 consecutive BID fractions → cumulative antigen release crosses ICD activation threshold
- Single 3.7 Gy fraction is below ICD optimal window (6-12 Gy)
- BID timing (6h gap) prevents tumor DNA repair between fractions
- Q4w spacing → T cell expansion and normal tissue recovery

## Normal Tissue Considerations

- Per-cycle BED₃: 33.1 Gy₃ (4 × 3.7 × (1+3.7/3))
- 4-week repair: ~40% recovery → net ~20 Gy₃ per cycle
- 3-cycle cumulative late EQD2: ~30-36 Gy
- Safe for prior RT ≤54 Gy with >1 year gap
